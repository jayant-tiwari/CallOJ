About Us



CallOJ:

  • Like any other institution for higher education, the International Institute of Information Technology, Bengaluru
    sees its fair share of coding enthusiasts every year. In this age, for an institution like IIITB which offers courses
    predominantly rooted in programming, everyone deserves to be a part of a community where we can learn and grow together
    through some friendly competition.

  • OnlineJudge is a Django based web application for people to prepare for interviews by honing their programming
    skills.

  • The reason it is any different than so many already existing platforms like Leetcode, GFG, etc. is that we can implement
    it on our college LAN and it would work seamlessly without the internet.

  • We can create a problem, make it available to everyone if you choose, you can create a contest to encourage people
    to participate, keep everyone busy by tracking their score on the Ranklist page and much more.


    • Playlist:

  • Playlist can be an archive store where you bookmarks all your problems. You can mark and tag your problems with
    different names and rate it accordingly.

  • In later versions of CallOJ, you will also be able to share this playlist with fellow CallOJer(s).
    CallOJ provides you a provision with directly adding a problem to one of the playlists.

  • Just like any other movie or song playlist, this feature is your one-stop solution for making an impact on the community.


    • Alerts:

  • CallOJ provides you with e-mail alerts on the night (IST) before the contest is scheduled to take place.
    This gives you an opportunity to plan your schedule and make space for this CallOJ contest.
